But I don't see a blog!

Never fear! I'm hosting my blog with dat:// instead of http://.

dat:// is designed for peer-to-peer browsing - essentially taking overpowered hosting companies out of the equation.

  1. Go get beaker browser (or another dat:// supporting browser).
  2. Open up dat://sandermoolin.com/blog in that browser.
  3. That's it!

You can also head back home if you'd prefer.
